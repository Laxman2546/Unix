Ford Mustang

The 1967 Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car, marking the first significant redesign of the original model. It featured a more aggressive and muscular appearance, with options for a powerful 390 cubic inch V8 engine. The 1967 Mustang is celebrated for its performance, style, and enduring influence on car culture..

Chevrolet Camaro

Chevrolet Camaro, produced from 1967 to 1969, is a classic American muscle car introduced to compete with the Ford Mustang. Known for its sleek design and powerful engine options, including the SS and Z/28 performance packages, the Camaro quickly became a favorite among car enthusiasts.
